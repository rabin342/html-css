Simple Omlettee Receipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omlettee combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total :10 minutes
  • Preparation:Approximately 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients

    • 2-3 large eggs
    • Salt, to taste
    • Pepper,to taste
    • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
    • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs.
